1 IMPORTANT POINTS

Q What is m sand?

A M sand is artificially made sand used in the construction as an eco friendly alternative to the natural river sand.

Q How is m sand manufactured?

A M sand is manufactured in following 3 steps


STEP 1 Hard stones are crushed using jaw crusher


STEP 2 Sand is made using sand making machine & is further sieved with vibrating screen


STEP 3 Sand is washed to remove dust particles

Q What are the advantages of using M sand?

A 1. Abundant Supply and eco Friendly Nature

2. Lesser Impurities

3. Excellent Strength and Better Utility

4. Cheaper in Nature







Q How M sand is sold?

A M sand is sold in the market in the unit of Metric ton. One ton concrete M sand is 21. 5 cubic foot in volume & one ton plastering M sand is 22 cubic foot in volume.


Q How M sand is delivered?

A Generally, M sand is delivered in two types of truck loads

  • 20 ton truck load delivered in 6 wheeler
  • 30 ton truck load delivered in 10 wheeler

Q How to store sand on site?

A Sand should be placed on a dry patch of ground and sand stacks should not be mixed with mud, clay or soil.

6 How to check quality of M sand on site?

Sand should not have organic matters like soil, clay and mud in it.
Sand sample can be checked by rubbing it with two fingers. If the fingers are stained, it means that sand contains clay.
Sand can also be added in a glass full of water. If water gets muddy, sand contains clay and soil.
