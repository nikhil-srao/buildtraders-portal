1 IMPORTANT POINTS

Q What is TMT?

A TMT bars are steel bars used in slabs, footings & concrete pillars while doing construction.

Q How TMT bars are made?

A TMT bars are made using the following 4 steps


STEP 1 Steel is recycled.


STEP 2 Iron billets are produced using recycled steel.


STEP 3 Billets are passed through rollers of decreasing diameter.


STEP 4 Formed steel bars are subjected to intensive cooling.

Q How TMT bars are sold in the market?

A TMT bars have many sizes ranging from 8mm to 32mm and each size is sold in the market in the form of bundles. Each size has different number of rods in the respective bundle. One buys desired number of bundles against each size. Finally, weight of all the bundles is calculated in tons and final cost is calculated based on the cost of TMT per ton.

4 STORAGE AND HANDLING

Q How to store TMT bars on site?

A Store on a dry patch of ground; away from clay, mud and water.
