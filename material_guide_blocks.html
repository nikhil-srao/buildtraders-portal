4 STORAGE AND HANDLING

Q How to store blocks on site?

A Blocks should be stored close to the construction site so that the their breakage in the transportation in minimal. The length of the stack should not be more than 3 meter and the width of the stack should not be more than 2 to 3 blocks.The height of the stack should not be more than 1 meter to avoid any defacement and breakage.They should be placed such that their is minimal contamination of soil, mud and other organic matters.

6 How to check quality of blocks on site?

Blocks should not break after dropping from a height of 1 meter.
One should observe carefully if blocks are made with properly graded aggregates. If properly graded aggregates are not used, block will have big cavities on its surface.
Blocks should not be brittle in nature. They should be properly cured.
