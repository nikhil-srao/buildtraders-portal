4 STORAGE AND HANDLING

Q How to store blocks on site?

A Blocks should be stored close to the construction site so that the their breakage in the transportation in minimal. The length of the stack should not be more than 3 mand the width of the stack should not be more than 2 to 3 blocks.
The height of the stack should not be more than 1m to avoid any defacement and breakage.
They should be placed such that their is minimal contamination of soil, mud and other organic matters.
