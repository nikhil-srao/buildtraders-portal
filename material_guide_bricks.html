1 IMPORTANT POINTS

Q How bricks are made?


A Bricks are made using four major steps.
1. Clay is prepared for making the bricks.
2.Either a mould or a wire cutter is used to create the shape of bricks.
3. They are kept for drying to remove the excess water.
4. They are burned where they gain their strength.





Q How bricks are sold in the market?


A Bricks are sold as an individual unit in the market. Generally, bricks are delivered in a truck load which can have about 3000 to 8000 bricks.


Q What is the standard size of bricks?


A The standard size of bricks : Length 9 inch; Width 4 inch; Height 3 inch.

Q What is the weight of a brick?


A A brick weighs about 3 to 4 kgs.

4 STORAGE AND HANDLING

Q How to store bricks on site?

A 1. Store bricks close to the construction so that breakage during transport is minimal.
2. Bricks should be stacked on dry grounds.
3. Bricks should not come in contact with mud, clay or soil.
4. The height of the stack should not be more than 10 bricks.

6 How to check quality of bricks on site?

Brick should not break when it is dropped from a height of 1m from the ground level.
Brick should not have organic matters and random lumps.
It should have a homogeneous in texture and should not have cracks.
